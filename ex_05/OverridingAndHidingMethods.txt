• Which method overrides a method in the superclass?
In ClassB, the method "methodTwo(int i)" overrides 
the method "methodTwo(int i)" in the superclass ClassA. 

• Which method hides a method in the superclass?
In ClassB, the method "methodOne(int i)" hides 
the method "methodOne(int i)" in the superclass ClassA.

• What do the other methods do?
The method "methodThree(int i)" in ClassA is a static method and 
the method "methodFour(int i)" in both classes are also static methods.
